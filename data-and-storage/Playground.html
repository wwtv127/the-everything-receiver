Rabbit R1 Control Playground (Inferred R1 Key Mappings)

How to Simulate (R1 Key Mappings):

  • **Scroll Wheel:** The R1 physical scroll is mapped to **`ArrowUp`** and **`ArrowDown`** key events.
  • **Side Button 1 (PTT):** The physical button is mapped to the **`P` key** (`KeyP`).
  • **Side Button 2 (Action):** The physical button is mapped to the **`A` key** (`KeyA`).

This environment listens for the keycodes (`ArrowUp`, `ArrowDown`, `KeyP`, `KeyA`) most commonly reported to be sent by the R1's hardware to the web view.
