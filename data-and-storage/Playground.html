R1 Keycode Detector & Debugger

Use this tool on your R1 to find the **exact keycodes** the physical buttons send.

How to Use:

  • Load this page on your Rabbit R1.
  • **Press and Hold PTT:** Note the codes that appear in the Status section.
  • **Press Action Button:** Note the codes that appear.
  • **Move Scroll Wheel:** Note the codes that appear.
  • The `event.code` is the value you need for your final game logic!
